INKED DINNER SERIES #23

 

You’ve probably seen them before, the misshapen carrots with their legs in a yoga twist or a parsnip that looks like a foot. What is the fate of these odd vegetables, of these misfits in the food pyramid?

 

Though they end up on your Instagram feed, they often don’t end up feeding anyone at all. So much imperfect produce is still perfectly edible yet it regularly goes to waste. Beauty comes in all shapes and sizes and the vegetables with blemishes and scars are no different.

 

Ugly Vegetables is a Vegan Dinner Experience  that respects and celebrates the fit to eat food that sprouts from the ground, whatever form they decided to come in. All of our produce for the vegan event will be grown and delivered by Emirates Bio Farm, to be enjoyed by you.


 

THE DETAILS

 

The Ugly Vegetable dinner experience will take place from January 15th until the 20th and there will be one seating each evening. Doors will open at 8pm and the dinner will start at 8:30pm sharp. The experience is priced at AED 360* per person and includes a 5-course menu and mocktails.

 

PLEASE NOTE THAT DUE TO THE PARTICULARITIES OF THE EVENT, THE MENU IS FIXED AND WE ARE UNABLE TO CATER TO FOOD ANY ALLERGIES & INTOLERANCES.

 

NOTE:  If you are coming as a group with different bookings and would like to be seated together, please inform us in advance.

 

* All prices are inclusive of mocktails, soft beverages, tea & coffee

 

 ** All prices are inclusive of VAT
