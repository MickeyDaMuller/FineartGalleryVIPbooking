Ramadan at Alserkal Avenue

Ramadan at Alserkal Avenue is a month filled with activities, exhibitions, pop-up iftars, musical evenings, and tours. Our popular Alserkal Lates take place every Wednesday during Ramadan, during which galleries and creative concepts open late, with tours taking place every Wednesday night.
